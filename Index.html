How i take on Any Project?

My Process

From wireframes to launch, below is a detailed look at how I run a typical project
Start

Brief To get a clear picture of what you need I start every project by receiving an initial brief of what you want. Having this gives me enough context to properly provide you with a proposal and ultimately tailor my services to your project goals Step 01

Initial Chat The next step is to have a quick chat up so we can properly introduce ourselves and make sure we're both on the same page. This is the perfect time to let me know anything I might need to be aware of during the project.

Wireframing Having a good set of wireframes is the bedrock of any successful website project. This is where we hammer down the layout and general functionality while always making sure the user is the primary focus. Step 02

Designs This is where I apply the paint to your website. I'll take the concepts we've agreed on in the wireframes to make them look usable, modern and clean.

↓

Code When the coding begins there will generally be less communication from me as this is quite an involved process that doesn't require much feedback. This is where I take the visuals of your website and make them into something real that people can interact with. Step 03

Step 04

Launch When it's time to launch the new site I'll take care of everything. All you'll need to do is get that Tweet ready about your shiny new website!

Training After the website has been coded. I will like to provide in-depth training. Going forward your website will be easily updatable by yourself so I'll take you through everything and even provide screencasts for you to keep.

End
